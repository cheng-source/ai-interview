InterviewState (统一状态)

  • candidate — 简历解析结果
  • currentStage — 当前阶段标识
  • techRound — 技术轮子状态
  • behavioralRound — 行为轮子状态
  • answerHistory[] — 全量回答+评估
  • scores — 实时累加评分
  • messages[] — 消息流(推前端)
  • qaCount — 反问计数

Checkpoint 检查点

  • 每个节点执行后自动存状态
  • 候选人断线 → 重连恢复
  • 支持人工审批节点 (interrupt)
  • 存储: PostgreSQL (langgraph checkpointer)

Conditional Edges (路由逻辑)

  • routeInTechnical — 追问/换题/进入行为轮
  • routeInBehavioral — 追问/换题/进入反问
  • shouldContinueQA — 继续反问/结束

核心数据模型

Candidate (候选人)

  • id, name, email, phone
  • resumeUrl, resumeParsed (JSON)
  • positionId (应聘岗位)
  • status: pending | interviewing | passed | rejected

Interview (面试记录)

  • id, candidateId, positionId
  • stateJson (LangGraph checkpoint)
  • threadId (LangGraph thread)
  • status, startedAt, endedAt

Question (题库)

  • id, title, type (technical|behavioral)
  • category, tags[], difficulty
  • expectedAnswer (参考答案)
  • evaluationCriteria (评分标准)

CompanyDoc (公司文档)

  • id, title, content, chunks[]
  • embedding (pgvector)
  • category, uploadedAt
  • 用于 RAG 检索

Report (面试报告)

  • id, interviewId, candidateId
  • techScore, behavScore, overallScore
  • strengths[], weaknesses[]
  • summary, recommendation

Position (岗位)

  • id, title, department
  • jdText (职位描述)
  • techStack[], level
  • questionTemplates (关联题库)
